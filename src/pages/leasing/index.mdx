# Leasing integrations

We offer several leasing integrations to help you sell your equipment online.

-   If you want minimal effort: embed a link to our calculator with our [button widget](./leasing/widgets/button) or our [calculator widget](./leasing/widgets/calculator)
-   To have maximum control over the look and feel, build your own custom leasing calculator using our [leasing API](./leasing/api)
