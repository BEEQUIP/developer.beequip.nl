# Changelog

Past and upcoming changes to our widgets.

Breaking changes are changes that will break behaviour of connecting clients. They will be announced 6 months
in advance to give clients ample time to make the required changes.

## Upcoming breaking changes

### 10 March 2026

- The domain `widgets.beequip.nl` will be removed and should be replaced with `widgets.beequip.com`.

## Past changes

### 4 May 2022

-   Fix button parameters `button-color`, `button-background-color` and `object-used`.

### 29 April 2021

-   Parameter `purchase-price` is now required.

### 16 October 2020

-   Change flow of calculating and requesting a lease.

### 13 October 2020

-   Change title above widget.

### 5 October 2020

-   Add support for dimensions in percentages.

### 23 September 2020

-   Fix display of incorrect down payment boundaries

### 8 September 2020

-   Add boundaries calculation boundaries
